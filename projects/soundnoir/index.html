Sound Noir

NYC Underground Dance Parties, 2009-2014

Design + Visuals

Sound Noir was a series of dance music events we produced across Brooklyn, Queens and Manhattan. With each event we worked to connect the dance music dots, from funk and soul, into disco then segueing into contemporary house and techno later in the night.

I was responsible for all things visual, including the flyers below, as well as producing then performing live visuals at the parties. For these visuals I leveraged VDMX for the performance of found and bespoke visual assets.
