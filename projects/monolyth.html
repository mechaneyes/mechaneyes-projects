Monolyth

Monolyth, 2022

A physical installation allowing for the discovery of art and artists

One interacts with the piece using gesture, for which I've leveraged a Leap Motion sensor. The piece is currently being developed as the vehicle for an ARG-enabled narrative.

This documentation is rough as the project's under active development.
