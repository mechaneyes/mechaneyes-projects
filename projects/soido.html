So I Do

TBD Festival, 2015

Design + Development + Installation

So I Do is a mobile web app I developed and installed at TBD Fest in Sacramento. It's a largely p5.js app that's networked to other phones running the app at the same time. The app operates as a communal drawing canvas whereby a mark made by one person on one phone is simultaneously displayed on all other phones running also running the app.

The app was projected nearly 50 feet across on the underside of a tent in a lounge space at the fetival. By intereacting with the app, the users were able to change their visual background and thus the entire mood and atmosphere there, under the tent.

Due to changes in iOS the app no longer plays nicely with iOS Safari out of the box. There are ways around this, so do reach out if you'd like me to share that with you.

You're welcome to use the app with any projector in any environment.
