Monolyth

Monolyth, 2022

A physical installation allowing for the discovery of art and artists

One interacts with the piece using gesture, for which I've leveraged a Leap Motion sensor. The MVP has a potential home at 21st and K streets in Sacramento. A person walking by will be able to browse through the gallery by waving her hand past the sensor. A QR code driving to the React Native app in development will allow them to explore further.

The piece is currently being further developed as the vehicle for an ARG-enabled narrative. Multiple Monolyths linked with the app, in addition to desktop+mobile websites, will drive the narrative forward. Inspiration comes from magic realism, film and games such as Year Zero.

The animation you see above is a p5js app running in realtime.
