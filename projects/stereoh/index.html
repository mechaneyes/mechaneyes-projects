Stereoh

Social VR Events, 2019-2020

As Stereoh, along with my 2 cofounders I produced a series of social VR events. The environments were built on an extended install of Mozilla Hubs with heavy customization of A-Frame and Three.js. We ran our own AWS servers to house the project.

I built our website in React, did much of the A-Frame and Three.js work as well as administered the AWS servers (dev+staging+prod).
